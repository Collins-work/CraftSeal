How It Works

From request to
completed job — simplified

A structured, technology-driven workflow that protects both clients and artisans at every step.

01

Post Your Job

Describe what you need — plumbing, electrical, carpentry, painting. Include photos, location, and any specific requirements.

02

Get Matched

Our system shows you verified artisans nearby ranked by specialty, ratings, portfolio quality, and availability.

03

Compare Quotes

Artisans bid on your job. Review quotations, check portfolios, read reviews, and choose who fits your budget and needs.

04

Secure Payment

Pay securely via KoraPay. Funds are held safely until the job is completed to your satisfaction. No disputes, no stress.

05

Rate & Review

After the job, rate your artisan. Your feedback builds the community reputation system that others rely on.
